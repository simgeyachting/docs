# Fresh Seafood: From Sea to Table

Coming from a family of fishermen, we take pride in offering our guests the freshest and most delicious seafood at all times. Throughout your day aboard our yacht, you'll have the opportunity to taste freshly caught fish and other seafood from the rich waters of the Aegean.

## Flavors from Our Fishing Heritage

For us, fishing is more than a profession — it's a way of life. Thanks to this heritage, we know exactly how to select, cook, and present the highest quality fish. Our menu is continuously updated with the freshest seasonal products.

At **Simge Yachting**, our goal is to give you not just a boat tour, but an unforgettable gastronomic experience.
