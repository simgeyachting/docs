# Our Yacht: Simge Yachting

The pride of the Simge Yachting family, **Simge Yachting** is a special vessel that combines the elegance of traditional Turkish gulets with modern luxury and comfort. Designed for unforgettable blue voyage experiences in the azure waters of the Aegean and Mediterranean, our yacht offers you the comfort of home on the sea.

![Simge Yachting Exterior](/images/galeri/simge001.jpg "Simge Yachting on the Blue Waters")

## Living Areas

### Cabins
We can accommodate up to 12 guests in our spacious 6 ensuite cabins. Each cabin features a private bathroom, toilet, air conditioning, and ample storage space. Our cabins are carefully designed for a peaceful night's sleep on the water.

### Salon
Our air-conditioned spacious salon features comfortable seating groups, TV, music system, and dining table. It offers a cozy retreat during bad weather or when you simply want to relax.

### Decks
Our yacht's spacious deck areas are perfect for enjoying the sun and sea. The foredeck has large sunbathing cushions, while the aft deck features a large dining table under shade and comfortable seating. This will be your favorite spot for watching the sunset.

![Simge Yachting Deck](https://res.cloudinary.com/djzzikcey/image/upload/v1752777147/momtur_uploads/q0dtoo6lsybfi9dsv2vb.jpg "Spacious and Comfortable Deck Area")

## Equipment & Activities

Our yacht is equipped with everything you need for a more enjoyable vacation:

- **Comfort:** Fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning throughout, Wi-Fi, music system in salon and deck.
- **Water Sports:** Service boat (Zodiac), 2 Paddleboards (SUP), 1 Canoe, Snorkel and Fins Sets, Fishing Tackle.

## Crew

Our professional and friendly crew of 4 is ready to take care of all your needs throughout your vacation. Our experienced captain knows the most beautiful coves, while our chef will prepare the most delicious Aegean cuisine for you. Your safety and comfort are our priority.

## Safety

Our yacht is equipped with all safety equipment (life jackets, life raft, fire extinguishers, etc.) in accordance with international maritime standards. Our crew undergoes regular safety training.
