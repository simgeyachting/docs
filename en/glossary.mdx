# Key Terms Glossary

At Simge Yachting, we have compiled some frequently used terms in the world of maritime and blue voyages to make your boat tour and yacht charter experience more understandable. Here are the basic concepts you need to know:

## A

### Anchoring

The process of a boat anchoring to fix itself at a specific location.

### Aquarium Bay

A popular Bodrum cove known for its crystal clear waters.

### All-Inclusive Boat Trip

A boat tour where additional services such as food and drinks are included in the tour price.

## B

### Bodrum Castle

An important tourist attraction reflecting Bodrum's historical and cultural richness.

### Bodrum Milas Airport

The nearest airport to Yalikavak and a starting point for boat tours.

## C

### Cabin

Private sleeping and living quarters on a yacht.

### Captain

The person responsible for the navigation, safety, and crew of the boat.

### Catamaran

A type of boat with two parallel hulls, generally offering a wider and more stable experience.

### Charter

The rental of a boat or yacht for a specific period.

### Cleopatra Beach

The famous beach on Sedir Island known for its unique sand and historical legend.

### Coxertme Cove

A calm fishing village cove known for its famous folk song.

### Crew

All personnel working on a boat, including the captain.

## D

### Deck

The upper part of the boat, an open area where one can walk and sunbathe.

### Daily Boat Tour

A one-day boat excursion, typically including swimming breaks and lunch.

## G

### Gulet

Traditional Bodrum wooden boats that combine both sailing and motor yacht features.

### Gulf of Gokova

A gulf located where the Aegean and Mediterranean meet, famous for its blue voyage routes.

### Gumusluk

A charming fishing village near Yalikavak, known for its ancient ruins and seafood restaurants.

## H

### Harbor

A natural or artificial sheltered place where ships and boats can dock, load/unload, shelter, and receive various services.

## I

### Island

A landmass surrounded by water, often visited during boat tours for swimming and exploration.

## K

### Karaada

One of Bodrum's popular islands, famous for its hot springs and natural pools.

### Knidos Ancient City

An important ancient city near Bodrum for history enthusiasts.

### Cove

A small, calm indentation where the sea extends into the land, usually sheltered from the wind.

### Kos

A Greek island reachable by boat tour from Bodrum, famous for its history and beaches.

## L

### Leros

A Greek island known for its calm landscapes, picturesque villages, and rich historical texture.

## M

### Marina

A harbor designed and equipped with modern facilities to meet the berthing, maintenance, repair, and other needs of yachts and boats.

### Moonlight Tour

A boat tour organized after sunset to enjoy the sea under the moonlight.

### Motor Yacht

A yacht powered primarily by engines rather than sails.

## O

### Orak Island

A popular island known as the "Maldives of Bodrum," with crystal clear waters and excellent snorkeling opportunities.

## P

### Patmos

A Greek island rich in spiritual and historical significance, known as the "Island of the Apocalypse."

### Private Boat Trip

A boat tour organized privately for a person or group, where the route and program can be determined upon request.

## R

### Route

The path or course a boat will follow during its voyage.

## S

### Sedir Island

An island in Gokova Gulf famous for Cleopatra Beach and ancient ruins.

### Snorkel

Equipment that allows breathing underwater, used to explore underwater beauty during boat tours.

### Sunset Boat Trip

A boat tour organized specifically to watch the sunset from the sea.

## T

### Turgutreis

A district on the western tip of the Bodrum Peninsula, known for its marina and sunset views.

## Y

### Yalikavak

A district located in the northwest of the Bodrum Peninsula, famous for its luxury marina and natural beauty.

### Yalikavak Marina

A world-class lifestyle center and harbor in Yalikavak with luxury boutiques, restaurants, and entertainment venues.

### Yacht Charter

The service of renting larger and more luxurious boats, usually with accommodation.
