# Bodrum Boat Tour Routes

Explore the most beautiful coves, islands, and historical sites of Bodrum with Simge Yachting. Whether you're planning a daily getaway or a weekly blue voyage — we offer the best route for you.

---

## Daily Routes

### Route 1: Classic Bodrum Tour
**Duration:** 8-10 hours | **Departure:** Yalikavak

```
Yalikavak → Orak Island → Karaada → Aquarium Bay → Koeck Adasi → Yalikavak
```

**Stops:**
- **Orak Island:** Swim break in turquoise waters
- **Karaada:** Thermal mud bath and cave visit
- **Aquarium Bay:** Snorkeling and swimming fun
- **Koeck Adasi:** Lunch and relaxation

> [Explore this tour](/tekne-turlari/gunluk-tekne-turlari)

### Route 2: Gokova Gulf Tour
**Duration:** 8-10 hours | **Departure:** Yalikavak

```
Yalikavak → Cirali Cove → Cicekli Cove → Incekum → Gokova Coves → Return
```

**Highlights:**
- **Cirali Cove:** Calm and clear sea
- **Cicekli Cove:** Natural beauty
- **Incekum:** Long sandy beach and swimming

### Route 3: Yalikavak Private Coves
**Duration:** 6-8 hours | **Departure:** Yalikavak Marina

```
Yalikavak → Palmiye Cove → Kisebuku → Yalikavak Coves → Return
```

**Highlights:**
- **Palmiye Cove:** Calm and peaceful atmosphere
- **Kisebuku:** Natural cove and swimming area

## Things to Consider When Choosing a Route

1. **Weather:** Wind and wave conditions are important in route planning
2. **Group Size:** Larger groups prefer wider routes
3. **Duration:** Choose a route length suitable for your time
4. **Interests:** History, nature, gastronomy — determine the route that suits you
5. **Season:** Longer routes are possible in summer months

## FAQ

### Can the itinerary be customized?
Absolutely. Routes are flexible based on weather conditions, sea state, and guest preferences.

### Is it suitable for gulets and luxury yachts?
Yes, all our routes work well for both gulet-type boats and luxury motor yachts. Short distances and sheltered bays make navigation easy.

### Is it family-friendly?
Definitely. Aquarium Bay and Orak Island offer calm, shallow waters ideal for families with children.

### Is there a nightlife option?
Our route focuses on nature and tranquility. Bodrum nightlife can be enjoyed before or after your tour.
