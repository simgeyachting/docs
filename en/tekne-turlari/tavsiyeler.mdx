# Boat Tour Tips

## Preparation

1. Weather Check
   - Check the day before for daily routes
   - Monitor weather for multi-day tours
   - Check wind speed and sea conditions

2. Essential Items
   - Sun protection:
     - High SPF sunscreen
     - Hat
     - Umbrella
     - Sunglasses
   - Clothing:
     - Light clothing
     - Waterproof shoes
     - Spare clothes
   - Personal items:
     - Camera/phone
     - Charger
     - Passport/ID
     - Health documents

3. Safety Precautions
   - Wear safety gear
   - Put on a life jacket
   - Talk to crew if you have a fear of the sea
   - Learn the emergency plan

## Behavior on Board

1. Safety
   - Walk carefully while the boat is moving
   - Stay away from moving parts
   - Follow crew instructions

2. Environmental Protection
   - Don't throw trash in the sea
   - Don't feed the fish
   - Don't touch the coral
   - Don't disturb local wildlife

3. Etiquette
   - Don't enter the crew's work area
   - Know your limits with alcohol consumption
   - Be quiet during night travel

## Route Selection

1. By Group Profile
   - Families: Aquarium Bay Route
   - Adventurers: Orak Island Route
   - Romantic couples: Moonlight Tours
   - Luxury travel: Private Yacht Route

2. By Season
   - Winter: Calmer routes
   - Mid-season: Popular coves
   - High season: Early booking
   - Low season: Discounted prices

## Extra Activities

1. Sea Activities
   - Jumping from the boat
   - Sea walking
   - Swimming
   - Snorkelling

2. Shore Activities
   - Beach visits
   - Local restaurants
   - Walking routes
   - Photo shooting

## Health Precautions

1. Sun Protection
   - Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours
   - Wear hat and sunglasses
   - Dress according to weather

2. Water Intake
   - Regular water consumption
   - Avoid alcoholic beverages
   - Get water at Incekum Cove

3. Health Conditions
   - Talk to crew if you have a fear of the sea
   - Keep your medication with you
   - Share information for emergencies
