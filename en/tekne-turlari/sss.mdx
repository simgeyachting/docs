# Frequently Asked Questions

## 📋 Reservation & Payment

### How do I make a reservation?
Contact us via WhatsApp or phone. Let us know your preferred tour type, date, and number of guests, and we will create your reservation.

### Is a deposit required?
Yes, a 25% deposit is required to confirm the reservation. The remaining balance is paid on the day of the tour.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Cash, credit card, and bank transfer.

## 🚢 Tours & Routes

### Where do we go on a daily tour?
We visit popular spots like Orak Island, Karaada, Koeck Adasi, and Aquarium Bay. See our [routes page](/en/tekne-turlari/rotalar) for detailed itineraries.

### How long do tours last?
Daily tours typically start at 10:00 AM and end around 6:00 PM (8-10 hours).

### Is food included?
Yes, lunch and beverages are included in our daily tours. Special dietary requests can be accommodated with advance notice.

## 💰 Pricing

### What are the 2026 season prices?
Visit our [pricing page](/en/tekne-turlari/fiyatlar) for current rates. Prices vary by season, boat type, and tour duration.

### Is a private tour available for 2 people?
Yes, we offer private tours for 2 people. The route, menu, and schedule are customized for you.

## ❌ Cancellation

### Can I cancel my reservation?
Free cancellation is available up to 7 days before the tour date. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable.

### What happens in bad weather?
Tours are rescheduled or an alternative date is offered with no penalty.
