At Simge Yachting, we invite you to discover the unique beauty of Bodrum Yalikavak with our daily boat tours. ## Daily Boat Tour Program and Route

Our boats depart from Yalikavak Marina every day, sailing towards the most popular and peaceful coves of the region. Our tour program is carefully designed to offer you maximum enjoyment and relaxation:

| Time | Activity |
|---
---|---|
| 10:00 | **Departure from Yalikavak Marina:** Take your place on our boat and begin our journey towards the cool waters of the Aegean Sea. |
| 10:30 | **First Swim Break — Kucukbuk Cove:** We take our first swim break at Kucukbuk Cove, famous for its crystal clear waters. Enjoy the refreshing sea. |
| 12:00 | **Lunch and Relaxation — Meteor Cove:** Anchoring at the calm and peaceful Meteor Cove, we serve a delicious lunch carefully prepared on board. Time for sunbathing and relaxation after the meal. |
| 14:00 | **Second Swim Break — Cennet Cove:** We take our second swim break at Cennet Cove, a corner of paradise as its name suggests. You'll feel rejuvenated in this cove's unique atmosphere. |
| 16:00 | **Afternoon Coffee and Snacks:** Towards sunset, shake off the day's fatigue with afternoon coffee and light snacks accompanied by pleasant conversations on board. |
| 17:30 | **Return to Yalikavak Marina:** After a day full of unforgettable memories, we return to Yalikavak Marina. |
