## Turgutreis

Located at the western tip of Bodrum, **Turgutreis** is famous for its spectacular sunsets and long sandy beaches.

### Places to Visit in Turgutreis

- **Turgutreis Marina (D-Marin)** — Modern marina with restaurants and cafes
- **Sunset Pier** — One of the Aegean's best sunset spots
- **Turgutreis Market** — Large Tuesday market
- **Aspat Castle** — Ancient ruins with panoramic views

### Turgutreis Beaches
- **Akyarlar Beach** — Long sandy beach, ideal for families
- **Kadıkalesi Beach** — Calm and clean waters
- **Bağla Cove** — Natural cove, accessible by boat

### Boat Tours from Turgutreis
Daily boats depart from Turgutreis to explore the most beautiful coves. You can also explore Turgutreis coves by departing from Yalikavak with Simge Yachting.

## Gümüşlük

Built on the ancient city of Myndos, **Gümüşlük** is famous for its historical atmosphere and tranquil vibe.

### Places to Visit in Gümüşlük

- **Ancient Myndos Ruins** — History dating back to 7th century BC
- **Gümüşlük Fishermen's Restaurants** — Fresh seafood dining
- **Rabbit Island (Tavşan Adası)** — Small island accessible on foot
- **Gümüşlük Jazz Festival** — Annual music festival

### Gümüşlük Beaches
- **Gümüşlük Town Beach** — Calm, family-friendly
- **Little Cove** — Quieter and more private

---

## Göltürkbükü

Known as the "Beverly Hills of Bodrum," **Göltürkbükü** is famous for luxury boutique hotels and world-renowned beach clubs.

### Places to Visit in Göltürkbükü

- **Maça Kızı** — World-famous beach club
- **Ada Beach** — Luxury beach experience
- **Büyük Koy** — Wide beach and clear water

---

## Torba

For those seeking a calm and peaceful atmosphere, **Torba** is popular for its proximity to Bodrum center.

- **Torba Beach** — Long sandy beach, calm waters
- **Torba Marina** — Small but charming marina
- **Close to Bodrum** — Just 10 minutes away

---

## Bodrum Center

### Places to Visit
- **Bodrum Castle (Underwater Archaeology Museum)** — City landmark
- **Bodrum Marina (Milta)** — Yacht marina and shopping
- **Ancient Theatre** — 2,300-year-old historical structure
- **Mausoleum of Halicarnassus** — One of the 7 Wonders of the Ancient World
- **Bodrum Bazaar** — Shopping and souvenirs
