# Paragliding: Meeting the Sky in Oludeniz

Your hands may tremble as you take your first run off a cliff, attached to a giant nylon wing. But when you meet the sky, soaring over the breathtaking scenery of Oludeniz, you will want to stop time with that strange calmness you feel. That moment when you feel you could stay in the sky forever explains why paragliding is such a special experience.

![Oludeniz Paragliding](/images/hero-dark.png "Paragliding Fun in Oludeniz")

## Why Oludeniz?

Oludeniz in Fethiye is considered one of the best paragliding centers not only in Turkey but also in the world. Here are a few reasons why:

- **The Magic of Babadag:** With its approximately 2000-meter summit, Babadag is a perfect launch point for paragliding. There are multiple launch pads suitable for different wind directions.
- **Unique Scenery:** From the moment you take off from Babadag, the Belcekiz Beach stretching beneath you and the famous Oludeniz lagoon's turquoise and navy blue tones will enchant you. Seeing Butterfly Valley and the surrounding islands from a bird's eye view is priceless.
- **Stable Weather Conditions:** The weather conditions that allow stable and safe flights for most of the year make Oludeniz attractive for both beginners and experienced pilots.
- **Professional Service:** Many professional paragliding companies with international certifications and experienced tandem pilots operate in the region. Your safety is always a priority.

## An Unforgettable Experience
The paragliding experience generally includes the following steps:

1.  **Preparation:** A pleasant journey from Oludeniz to the summit of Babadag by private vehicle.
2.  **Takeoff:** Your experienced pilot gives you a short briefing. After a few steps of running, your feet leave the ground and you find yourself in the vast sky.
3.  **Flight:** During the approximately 25-40 minute flight, you enjoy the 360-degree panoramic view accompanied by your pilot. If you wish, your pilot can even perform adrenaline-filled acrobatic moves.
4.  **Landing:** The flight ends with a smooth landing on Oludeniz's famous Belcekiz Beach.
