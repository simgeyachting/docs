# Places to Visit in Yalikavak

Located on the northwest coast of the Bodrum Peninsula, **Yalikavak** is one of the most prestigious destinations in the region, known for its luxury marina, boutique hotels, and natural beauty. Here are the must-visit places in Yalikavak:

## 1. Yalikavak Marina

One of the world's most prestigious marinas, **Yalikavak Marina** is famous for its luxury boutiques, Michelin-starred restaurants, and vibrant nightlife.

- 🛍️ **Shopping:** Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Hermès and other luxury brands
- 🍽️ **Dining:** Fine-dining restaurants with sea views
- 🚤 **Yachts:** Home to some of the world's largest megayachts
- 🌅 **Sunset:** Breathtaking sunset views over the marina

## 2. Yalikavak Beaches

### Yalikavak Public Beach
Free entry, sunbed and umbrella rental, cafe, and shower facilities.

### Sands Beach Club
Private beach club with sunbeds, restaurant, and bar. Entry fee applies.

### Palmarina Beach
Private beach within the marina, daily entry options available.

## 3. Coves and Natural Beauty

Stunning coves accessible by boat around Yalikavak:

- **Akvaryum Cove** — Crystal clear water, perfect for snorkeling
- **Tavşan Burnu** — Peaceful swimming spot
- **Poyraz Cove** — Natural cove sheltered from the wind
- **Çökertme Cove** — Famous for its untouched natural beauty

> 💡 **Tip:** The best way to explore these coves is with a [daily boat tour with Simge Yachting](/en/tekne-turlari/gunluk-tekne-turlari).

## 4. Dining

### Fine Dining
- **Mimoza Restaurant** — Seafood and Aegean cuisine
- **Kitchen By Blue** — Modern fusion cuisine
- **Lemon Tree** — Light snacks and breakfast

### Local Flavors
- **Yalıkavak Balıkçısı** — Fresh fish and meze
- **Sardunya** — Traditional Bodrum dishes

## 5. Shopping

Beyond luxury brands, Yalikavak offers boutique shops with handmade products, olive oil, and local souvenirs.

- **Yalikavak Marina Boutiques** — Luxury brands
- **Yalikavak Bazaar** — Local products and souvenirs
- **Olive Oil Shops** — Cold-pressed Aegean olive oils

## 6. Yalikavak Sailing Club

Ideal for water sports enthusiasts: sailing, windsurfing, canoeing, and more.

## 7. Nearby Destinations

- **Bodrum Center** — 20 minutes by car
- **Gümüşlük** — 10 minutes by car, ancient Myndos city
- **Göltürkbükü** — 15 minutes by car
- **Turgutreis** — 25 minutes by car
