# Bodrum Coves: Hidden Paradises in the Jewel of the Aegean

<img src="/images/galeri/simge005.jpg" alt="Bodrum Coves" style={{borderRadius: "12px", marginBottom: "24px", width: "100%" }} />


Bodrum is famous not only for its lively nightlife and historical texture but also for its beautiful coves with crystal clear waters. During boat tours with Simge Yachting, you will have the opportunity to explore these unique coves, swim in the deep blue waters, and enjoy nature. Here are some popular coves you must see in and around Bodrum:

## 1. Orak Island

One of Bodrum's most popular boat tour stops, Orak Island is like an aquarium with its Maldives-like turquoise sea. It is a perfect place for snorkeling and exploring underwater life. The calm atmosphere of the island is ideal for those seeking a peaceful day.

## 2. Aquarium Bay

As its name suggests, Aquarium Bay is famous for its clear, glass-like sea. Since underwater visibility is very high, you can experience swimming with fish. It is an indispensable stop especially for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.

## 3. Karaada

One of the closest islands to Bodrum, Karaada is known for its healing mud baths. The mineral-rich mud from the island's cave is believed to be beneficial for the skin. The coves around the island are also very suitable for swimming and relaxation.

## 4. Bagla Cove

Located south of Bodrum, Bagla Cove stands out with its long and wide sandy beach. With its calm sea and sheltered structure, it is a preferred cove especially on windy days. It is also a very suitable option for families.

## 5. Camel Beach

Named after the camels found on the beach, Camel Beach is a popular stop with both its sandy beach and sea. Here you can ride camels, do water sports, or simply enjoy the sun. It is especially preferred by young people and those seeking entertainment.

## 6. Aspat Cove

Standing out with its historical texture and natural beauty, Aspat Cove has a calm and peaceful atmosphere. With its ancient ruins and lush nature, it offers visitors a different experience. It is one of the quiet stops on boat tours.

## 7. Cennet Cove

One of Bodrum's most prestigious coves, Cennet Cove brings luxury and nature together. It is known for hosting world-famous hotels like Mandarin Oriental and dazzles with its turquoise waters. [Click for more](/destinasyonlar/cennet-koyu).
