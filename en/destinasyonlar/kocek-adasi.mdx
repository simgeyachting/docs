# Koeck Adasi: Bodrum's Hidden Cove

Koeck Adasi is one of the most popular boat tour stops on the Bodrum Peninsula. With its crystal clear turquoise waters, calm coves, and natural beauty, it is an indispensable destination for daily boat tours. Frequently visited on daily tours organized by Simge Yachting, Koeck Adasi is ideal especially for swimming and snorkeling enjoyment.

## Where is Koeck Adasi?

Koeck Adasi is located southwest of Bodrum, off the coast of Yalikavak. It is approximately 20-30 minutes by boat from Yalikavak Marina or Bodrum center. The area around the island is surrounded by calm coves where boat tours anchor for swimming breaks.

## How to Get to Koeck Adasi?

Transportation to Koeck Adasi is only possible by sea:

- **Daily Boat Tours:** Daily boat tours departing from Yalikavak and Bodrum center visit Koeck Adasi
- **Private Boat Charter:** You can explore the island on your own schedule by chartering a private boat with Simge Yachting
- **Blue Voyage:** Koeck Adasi is a frequently preferred stop on weekly blue voyage routes

## Things to Do at Koeck Adasi

### Swimming and Snorkeling
The clear and calm waters of Koeck Adasi are perfect for swimming and snorkeling. The underwater life is very rich; you can observe colorful fish and marine creatures.

### Nature Walk
The island is covered with olive trees and maquis. With a short walk, you can climb to the top of the island and watch Bodrum's unique view.

### Photography
The view offered by Koeck Adasi at sunset is an unmissable opportunity for photography enthusiasts.
