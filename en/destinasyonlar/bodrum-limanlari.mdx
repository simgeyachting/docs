# Bodrum Harbors: Sea Gateways in the Heart of the Aegean

<img src="/images/galeri/simge004.jpg" alt="Bodrum Harbors" style={{borderRadius: "12px", marginBottom: "24px", width: "100%" }} />


Bodrum, as the jewel of the Aegean Sea, stands out not only for its natural beauty and historical texture but also for its modern and comprehensive harbors. Strategically located for yacht tourism and maritime, Bodrum's harbors are favorites of both local and international sailors and vacationers. At Simge Yachting, we actively use these harbors for our boat tours and yacht charter services. Here are the main harbors of Bodrum and the facilities they offer:

## 1. Milta Bodrum Marina

Located in Bodrum city center, Milta Bodrum Marina is one of the region's oldest and best-known marinas. Thanks to its central location, marina guests can easily access Bodrum's lively atmosphere, historical sites, restaurants, and shopping centers.

**Features and Services:**

*   **Capacity:** Extensive berthing capacity both at sea and on land.
*   **Technical Service:** All kinds of maintenance and repair services.
*   **Social Areas:** Restaurants, cafes, boutiques, and supermarket.
*   **Security:** 24-hour security and surveillance.
*   **Transportation:** Close proximity to city center and bus terminal.

Milta Bodrum Marina is an ideal option for both short visits and long-term berthing.

## 2. Yalikavak Marina

Located in the Yalikavak district on the western side of the Bodrum Peninsula, Yalikavak Marina is considered one of the world's most luxurious and prestigious marinas. It is internationally recognized for its capacity to host mega yachts and its top-tier services.

**Features and Services:**

*   **Capacity:** Extensive berthing capacity including mega yachts.
*   **Shopping and Entertainment:** Stores of world-famous brands, gourmet restaurants, bars, and nightclubs.
*   **Accommodation:** Luxury hotels and residences.
*   **Health and Beauty:** Spa centers and gyms.
*   **Technical Service:** Comprehensive technical support and maintenance services.

Yalikavak Marina offers a unique experience for those seeking luxury and comfort combined.

## 3. D-Marin Turgutreis

Located at the western tip of the Bodrum Peninsula, D-Marin Turgutreis stands out with its calm and peaceful atmosphere. Serving at international standards, the marina is preferred especially by sailors looking for long-term stays and a quieter environment.

**Features and Services:**

*   **Capacity:** Extensive berthing capacity and haul-out area.
*   **Technical Service:** Comprehensive maintenance and repair workshops.
*   **Social Areas:** Restaurants, cafes, market, and banking services.
*   **Transportation:** Close to Turgutreis center with public transport options.
*   **Environment:** A quiet location surrounded by natural beauty.

D-Marin Turgutreis is a harbor that both meets maritime needs and offers a peaceful holiday opportunity.
