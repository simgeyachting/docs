## 1. Aquarium Cove

**Bodrum's clearest waters.** Glass-like water, fish schools. Underwater visibility up to 30 meters. Perfect for snorkeling.

---

## 2. Karaada Hot Springs

**Natural thermal experience.** 30-35°C water temperature. Good for rheumatism and skin conditions. Anchor at Capa Cove or Poyraz Harbor.

---

## 3. Cokertme

**Traditional fishing village.** Famous fish restaurants, fresh seafood, castle ruins.

---

## 4. Orak Island

**Boat tour favorite.** Small sandy beach, turquoise waters, swimming and picnicking.

---

## 5. Yalikavak Coves

**Marina and cove combination.** Tilkicik (sandy beach, beach clubs), Bagla (family-friendly, shallow water), Kudur (town center, free).

---

## 6. Gumusluk Cove

**Full of ancient ruins.** Ancient city of Myndos, shallow water (walk to the island), sunset views, live music restaurants.

---

## 7. Turkbuku Cove

**Bodrum's Saint Tropez.** VIP beach clubs, luxury bars, nightlife.

---

## 8. Cennet Cove (Paradise Cove)

**The name says it all.** Surrounded by pine forests, camping possible. Difficult to reach by road — going by boat is the best option.

---

## 9. Kisebuku (Kargi Cove)

**Bodrum's longest beach.** 1 km sandy beach, beach restaurants, water sports.

---

## 10. Sedir Island (Cleopatra Beach)

**Legendary sands.** Sand reportedly brought from Egypt for Cleopatra. Cedreae ancient ruins. Protected area — sunscreen prohibited on beach.
