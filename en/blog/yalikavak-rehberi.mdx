## Palmarina Yalikavak

**Turkey's largest and most luxurious marina.** 620-berth capacity, megayacht dock, 75+ shops and restaurants, art galleries.

### What to Do at the Marina

**Shopping** — Luxury brands, local designers, souvenir shops

**Dining** — Seafront restaurants, cocktail bars, coffee shops

**Events** — Summer concerts, art exhibitions, boat shows

---

## Yalikavak Beaches

### Tilkicik Cove — Most popular beach. Sandy, calm waters, beach clubs
### Bagla Cove — Ideal for families. Shallow water, wide sandy beach
### Kudur Park — Town beach. Free entry, children's playground
### Gundogan — 10 min from Yalikavak. Long sandy beach, sunset views

## Places to Visit

**Yalikavak Windmills** — 18th-century windmills, hilltop panorama, free entry

**Yalikavak Market** — Every Thursday. Fresh produce, local cheeses, handmade crafts

**Dibeklihan Art Village** — Art galleries, workshops, cafe, concerts
