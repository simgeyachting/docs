## Indian Wedding Program

### Day 1: Mehndi (Henna Night)
Professional henna artists, traditional Indian music, dance performances

### Day 2: Sangeet (Music Night)
Live music or DJ, choreographed dance performances, family acts

### Day 3: Haldi (Purification Ceremony)
Turmeric ceremony for bride and groom. Close family participation.

### Day 4: Wedding (Main Ceremony)
Baraat (groom's procession), Kanyadaan, Saptapadi (7 steps), Mangalsutra

### Day 5: Reception (Welcome Party)
Buffet dinner, live music, dance floor, cake cutting
