## Kid-Friendly Routes

### 1. Yalikavak Coves Tour

**The best option.** Calm waters, short distance, shallow beaches.

- Tilkicik — Sandy beach
- Bagla — Shallow water
- Kudur — Close, convenient

  title="Tour Duration"

### 2. Karaada Tour

**Hot springs experience.** Thermal water is fun for kids. Caution: hot springs may be too warm for small children.

### 3. Orak Island

**Crystal waters.** Clear water, you can see fish. Perfect for snorkeling. Water is deep — life jacket required for non-swimmers.

## Safety Tips

<Card
  title="Life Jacket Required"
  icon="life-ring"
  body="Keep a properly sized life jacket for every child. Always wear on deck."
/>

**On Board:** No running, no leaning over railings, stay away from captain's area

**In the Water:** Start in shallow water, always supervise, teach swimming with life jacket

## What to Bring

**Ages 0-3:** Diapers, wet wipes, baby food, sun hat, toys, sleep blanket

**Ages 9-12:** GoPro, snorkel set, card games, book

**General:** Sunscreen (SPF 50+), sunglasses, hat, light cover-up, medications

## FAQ

### What if my child gets seasick?
Give medication 1 hour before the tour. Sit in the middle of the deck, look at the horizon.

### Can I bring a baby on a boat tour?
Yes, but choose shorter tours (4-5 hours). Keep in shade, give plenty of water.

### Is it suitable for non-swimming children?
Yes, with a life jacket. They can walk into the water at shallow coves.
