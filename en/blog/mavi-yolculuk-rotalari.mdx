## Day 1: Bodrum - Karaada

**Karaada** — Natural hot water springs. Good for rheumatism and skin conditions. Anchor at Capa Cove or Poyraz Harbor.

---

## Day 2: Karaada - Cokertme

**Cokertme** — Bodrum's best fish restaurants. Fresh seafood, quiet harbor, castle ruins.

---

## Day 3: Cokertme - Sedir Island

**Sedir Island (Cedreae)** — The legendary Cleopatra Beach. Special sand reportedly brought from Ancient Egypt. Temple of Apollo, ancient theater, Byzantine church.

  title="Cleopatra Beach"

---

## Day 4: Sedir Island - English Harbor

**English Harbor** — The most sheltered cove in Gokova. Can anchor year-round. Surrounded by pine forests.

---

## Day 5: English Harbor - Akbuk

**Akbuk** — Modern facilities and natural beauty. Restaurants, cafes, clean beaches. Markets and pharmacies available for supplies.

---

## Day 6: Akbuk - Seven Islands

**Seven Islands** — The jewel of Gokova. Kucuk Island, Zeytinli Island, Uzun Island, Marti Island, Tavsan Island, Yassi Island, Kirmizi Island. Island hopping, snorkeling, fishing.

---

## Day 7: Seven Islands - Bodrum

**Return day.** Final swimming break, breakfast, luggage preparation. 4-5 hour journey back to Bodrum.
