## Factors Affecting Prices

### 1. Yacht Type and Size

| Yacht Type | Capacity | Daily Price Range |
|------------|----------|-------------------|
| Gulet (12m) | 8-12 guests | ₺15,000 - ₺25,000 |
| Gulet (18m) | 12-16 guests | ₺25,000 - ₺40,000 |
| Motor Yacht | 6-10 guests | ₺30,000 - ₺80,000 |
| Sailboat | 4-8 guests | ₺12,000 - ₺20,000 |

### 2. Season Periods

**High Season (June - September)**
- Prices 30-50% higher
- Early booking recommended

**Mid Season (May - October)**
- More affordable prices
- Ideal weather conditions

**Low Season (November - April)**
- Most economical option
- Some yachts under maintenance

### 3. Included Services

**Standard Package Typically Includes:**
- ✅ Crew (captain, sailor)
- ✅ Fuel (for designated route)
- ✅ Insurance
- ✅ Cleaning

**May Cost Extra:**
- Lunch and beverages
- Lunch (₺200-400 / person)
- Port fees
- Water sports equipment

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I negotiate yacht charter prices?

Standard tour prices are fixed. Private charters may offer flexibility based on season and duration.

### Is food included in the price?

Daily tours typically include lunch. Private charters offer meal package options.

### What size yacht should I charter?

Up to 6 guests: 12m gulet is sufficient. 8-12 guests: 16-18m yacht recommended.
