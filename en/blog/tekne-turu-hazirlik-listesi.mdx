## Daily Boat Tour List

### Essential
- [ ] Swimsuits (2)
- [ ] Beach towel (quick-dry)
- [ ] Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
- [ ] Sunglasses
- [ ] Hat (wide-brimmed)

### Recommended
- [ ] Water shoes
- [ ] Light sweater
- [ ] Waterproof phone case
- [ ] Cash
- [ ] Medications (seasickness, pain reliever)

### Entertainment
- [ ] GoPro / waterproof camera
- [ ] Snorkel set
- [ ] Music player + headphones
- [ ] Book

  title="Daily Tour Tip"
  icon="lightbulb"

## Blue Cruise List (3-7 Days)

### Clothing
- [ ] Swimsuits (3-4)
- [ ] Beach towels (2)
- [ ] Pareo / beach dress
- [ ] T-shirts (4-5)
- [ ] Shorts (3-4)
- [ ] Dress / skirt (1-2)
- [ ] Pajamas
- [ ] Sweater / jacket
- [ ] Sneakers + sandals + flip-flops

### Personal Care
- [ ] Toothbrush and toothpaste
- [ ] Shampoo + body wash
- [ ] Sunscreen (SPF 50+, plenty)
- [ ] After-sun lotion
- [ ] SPF lip balm
- [ ] Deodorant

### Health
- [ ] Seasickness pills
- [ ] Pain reliever + fever reducer
- [ ] Antihistamine
- [ ] Prescription medications
- [ ] Band-aids + antiseptic cream
- [ ] Insect repellent

### Electronics
- [ ] Phone + charger
- [ ] Power bank
- [ ] Headphones
- [ ] Camera + memory card
- [ ] Waterproof case

### Documents
- [ ] ID / passport
- [ ] Health insurance card
- [ ] Reservation documents

---

## For Children

**Ages 0-6:** Diapers, wet wipes, baby food, bottle, toys, sun hat

**Ages 7-12:** Swim aids, mask and snorkel, card games, tablet

---

## Seasickness Prevention

<Card
  title="Take Precautions"
  icon="warning"
  body="Take medication 1 hour before the tour starts."
/>

- Dramamine, Bonamine pills
- Seasickness wristband
- Ginger gum / drink
- Sit in the middle of the deck, look at the horizon

## Luggage Tips

<Card
  title="Choose a Soft Bag"

- ❌ Hair dryer (available on yachts)
- ❌ Heavy valuables
- ❌ Glass bottles
- ❌ Excessive makeup
