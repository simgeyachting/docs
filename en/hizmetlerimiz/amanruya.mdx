# Where Luxury Meets Tranquility in Bodrum

![Amanruya Luxury Accommodation](https://res.cloudinary.com/dy3xe3ver/image/upload/v1752932340/495086543_1237221831081470_3518613198927664901_n_n8gcnh.jpg)

Nestled within Bodrum's unique natural beauty on a magnificent hillside overlooking the Aegean Sea, this exclusive resort is a haven of luxury and tranquility. At Simge Yachting, we offer private boat tours and yacht charter services to guests of this resort, combining this exceptional accommodation experience with maritime adventure.

## Exclusive Accommodation Experience

The resort features private stone villas that blend traditional Turkish architecture with modern luxury. Each villa boasts a private pool, spacious terraces, and breathtaking Aegean Sea views, offering guests complete privacy and serenity.

**Features & Services:**

*   **Private Villas:** Luxury stone villas, each with a private pool and sea view.
*   **Gourmet Restaurants:** Select flavors from Turkish and international cuisine.
*   **Spa & Wellness:** Traditional Turkish bath, massage, and yoga sessions.
*   **Library & Art Gallery:** A rich library and gallery showcasing local artists' work.
*   **Private Beach:** A calm and peaceful beach area.
